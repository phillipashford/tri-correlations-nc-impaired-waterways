A Placebased Cost of Living Analysis for GIS Analysts

University of Kentucky Geography, New Maps Plus, MAP671 Final Project

Check out the repository here!

The map derived from this research will visualize the ratio between on-location (non-remote) gis job salaries and the cost of living (COLI) across dozens of US cities. My intent is to identify the places with the highest ratio of salary to COLI (ie, the most potentially lucrative places to live as a GIS pro working on-site).

I'm currently using python, jupyter notebooks, and pandas to customize a scraper to pull Google Jobs data (salary based on title and location). See progress here.

The legend (in progress) will include a color gradient to indicate the ratio between salary and cost of living in a given area. I am going to try to use the code I found here to create a customized legend in Mapbox!
